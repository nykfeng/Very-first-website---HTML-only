Coffee Beans

  1. Coffea Arabica (Arabica)
    This species makes up about 70% of the world market for coffee beans. Being a higher value taste, this fancy grade coffee bean is sold mostly through specialty coffee shops.

  2. Coffea canephora (Robusta)
    This high caffeine content species makes up about 27% of the world coffee bean market. Being a cheaper to produce coffee bean but with a lower taste profile, this coffee is often sold for use in instant coffee.

  3. Coffea liberica Bull. ex Hiern.
    This species, grown in Malaysia, Liberia and the Ivory Coast, makes up about 3% of the world coffee bean market.
